Patients must fully disclose their medical history, including systemic illnesses, allergies, medications, pregnancy status, and prior dental treatments. Accurate disclosure is essential for safe and effective dental care.

Informed consent is obtained before any procedure. Consent may be written, verbal, or digital, and emergency situations may require immediate treatment without prior written consent to protect patient health.
